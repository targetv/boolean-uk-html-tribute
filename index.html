About Mumford and Sons

Mumford & Sons are an English folk rock band formed in London in 2007. The band consists of Marcus Mumford (lead vocals, electric guitar, acoustic guitar, drums), Ben Lovett (vocals, keyboards, piano), Winston Marshall (vocals, electric guitar, banjo) and Ted Dwane (vocals, bass guitar, double bass).

  • 2011 Emerging Artists of the Year
  • 2011 Top Alternative Artist
